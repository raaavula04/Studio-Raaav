About the Game

The Law Has Fur.

In a sun-baked suburban backyard, something has gone terribly, adorably wrong. The lizards have gone rogue. The neighbourhood squirrels have formed a gang. And the only thing standing between total backyard anarchy and a quiet Tuesday afternoon is you — a tiny, magnificently-hatted sheriff cat.

Paw & Order is a cozy-action hybrid that weaponises the mundane. A garden hose becomes a canyon river. A stack of cardboard becomes a saloon. Every plastic toy becomes a relic of frontier legend.

Designed to be finished. Designed to be remembered. 2–4 hours of pure backyard justice.

Cozy Action Top-Down 2D Pixel Art PC · Steam Solo Dev
