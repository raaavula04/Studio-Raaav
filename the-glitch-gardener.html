About the Game

The System Has a Bug.
That Bug is You.

You are a virus loose inside a dying computer. The antivirus programs hunt you relentlessly — but when you destroy them, they drop corrupted seeds. Plant those seeds. Grow the glitch.

The Glitch Gardener is a retro-digital survival game where chaos is your resource and disorder is your weapon. The messier the system gets, the stronger you become.

Survive the periodic system purges. Outlast the cleanup routines. Let the garden spread until nothing is left to save.

Top-Down Survival Retro Digital Pixel Art PC · Itch.io Solo Dev
